About the Project

We trained a Deep Convolutional GAN (which took a really long time) on a little over 60,000 training images from popular Disney movies & TV shows. Given the short time frame for the Hackathon, we made use of many libraries including PyTorch and Fastai which aided in our develpment process. If you look below, you can see our results!
